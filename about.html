About Me

Hello, I am Joshua Leicht. I am a senior studying mechanical engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Throughout my time at the school, I have gotten the opportunity to design numerous projects on CAD software and implement those designs using modern manufacturing techniques. At my internship, I was able to design a wiping mechanism to implement to their existing platform using traditional manufacturing techniques. Additionally, through my classes I have a basic understanding of fluid dynamics, thermodynamics, and coding to allow me to quickly adapt to my surroundings. My technical skills include Fusion 360, SolidWorks, Inventor, Python, and Excel. In my free time I enjoy powerlifting, playing sports, and singing in the choir.
